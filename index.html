Chacarera

The chacarera is one of the most famous genres in the argentinian music. It is from the state of Santiago del Estero. Is a very dinamic style with a very happy dance. The key could be writted, at the same way of the mostly argentinian's folk music, in 3/4 or 6/8. It has two parts, and its form is a|b|a|b|a|b|b x 2. There are three types of chacarera


  • simple
  • doble
  • trunca
